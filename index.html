Problem

Challenge Description

Next Reality – Develop innovative and fascinating mixed reality offers/features (AR, VR) for the FC Bayern Community

What’s the problem/ task?

While the Internet has allowed Bayern Munich to grow a global following, the in-arena experience has remained sadly isolated due to geographical restrictions. We believe that with the advent of Virtual Reality the stadium experience can be available to everyone around the world, in the comfort of their own homes.

Solution

Prototype

Our solution takes footage from the stadium and allows it to be streamed live in 4K resolution to a mobile device placed in a VR headset. This, combined with high quality sound recordings will allow accurate emulation of the stadium atmosphere on a match day.

Uniqueness

Our solution allows a fan to experience the spine tingling atmosphere of being in the stadium, but delivered to you in the comfort and convenience of your own home.
